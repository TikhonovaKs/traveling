The Li River

URL

national-parks.org

Guilin and Lijiang River National Park is located in the northeastern region of Guangxi Zhuang, China, and features the Li River, also known as Li Jiang, of the Pearl River Basin. The river flows a total of 102 miles (164 km).

The national park represents the outstanding scenery along the Li River between the cities of Yangshuo and Guilin City. The scenery found between these two cities even serves as the artwork on the Chinese 20 yuan note. The mountains decorate the shoreline as they individually pierce toward the sky.

Winter Perm Krai

URL

go.mishka.travel/winterperm

An unusual region for the average tourist that is gaining popularity, all while being just a 2-hour flight from Moscow!

Dangle your legs from the rugged cliffs, gazing at the vast and mighty Ural landscapes. Embark on snowmobiles through the enchanting winter forest to the summit of Mount Polyud. To ascend to the Commemorative Stone, a place rarely reached even by seasoned travelers during winter.

Kamchatka

URL

go.mishka.travel

Kamchatka. Kamchatka crabs, caviar, scallops, sockeye salmon, and other seafood... And what about roasting bananas on lava?

Volcanoes, volcanoes, lava fields, all mixed with black beaches. Mars, the Moon, and other extraterrestrial landscapes. Kamchatka is both a wild Iceland and a remote Alaska. There is no other place like it on the planet. It is unique.

Unique Nepal

URL

ntb.gov.np

National color, a culture that spans several centuries, the absence of megacities, people still living in harmony with nature, and, of course, the Himalayan mountains!

Nepal rightfully holds the title of the "roof of the world": in this small mountainous country, there are 8 out of the 14 eight-thousanders on the planet! Nepal is the "home" of the world's highest peak - Mount Everest - and the birthplace of Gautama Buddha.
